✍️ Prompt of the Week

The listing description that gets showings

Copy this exactly. Paste it into Claude or ChatGPT. Fill in the brackets with your property details.

Copy this prompt

"You are a luxury real estate copywriter. Write a compelling MLS listing description for a [number] bedroom, [number] bathroom home in [city/neighborhood]. Key features: [list your features]. Target buyer: [describe them]. Tone: aspirational but grounded. Maximum 250 words. Open with one sentence that creates an emotional image of living in the home."

Save this prompt. Use it on every listing from here on. Most agents find it saves 30 to 45 minutes per listing and produces stronger copy than they write manually.

💬 Handle This Objection

"We're waiting for rates to drop."

The number one thing agents are hearing right now. Here is a script to try — adapt it to your own voice and market:

"That makes complete sense. Here is what I have seen happen though — when rates drop, every buyer who has been waiting jumps in at the same time. More competition, higher prices, bidding wars. The buyers winning right now are the ones who move before the crowd arrives. Would it help if I showed you what the numbers actually look like for your budget at today's rates versus six months from now?"

This is a conversational script — a starting point, not something to memorize word for word. Adapt it to your voice and your client's specific situation.
